Welcome To

The Isle of Dern

As far as the eye can see.

Regions

The Isle of Dern is made up from various regions, bring colour and variation to our game world of epic proportions. Explore and master each region, discover their secrets, inhabitants and opportunities, experiencing all outcomes.

Highlands

The largest region features barren rolling mountains, sharp coastlines and aged high-altitude forests.

Uplands

Overwhelmingly barren and populated with desolate moorland, the uplands are broken up by great lakes and adjacent woodland.

Lowlands

Defined as the rift between the Highlands and Uplands, the lowlands feature rich contrast between harsh geographic valleys and fissures, great plains, swampland, and rolling hills.

Forests

From ancient redwood forests to subtle sapling woodland, the Isle of Dern features diverse and beautiful forestry throughout.

Coasts

Coastlines surround The Capital in the center of the game world, staggered ridges and exposed headlands ride the edges of the mountains in the highlands. Pebbled beaches house the great majority of the islands crab population.

Isles

In the southern reaches of the Isle of Dern, the landscape can be fully experienced through the aggressive and rugged terrain of the Isles, featuring narrow lochs, exposed peninsulas and sharp outcrops.

Moorland

Left behind in the soft wet soil, the great plains of moorland tell the sorry tales of failed missions, abandoned convoys and sorrow remains.

Feature Locations

Notable locations across the Isle of Dern.

The Capital's Island

Standing proud in the center of the Isle, the Tower marks The Capital's oppressive reign and dominion, but also its function of fundamental safety, progression and expression. Surrounded by a far reaching estuary and built on solid bedrock. The Capital is known to be the only official embassy on the Isle of Dern and can be seen from the furthest reaches of the island. Responsible for the governing and the operation of the Proving Grounds, The Capital relies on technological advantages to keep of mortal warring factions that wage against its service to the games.

The Last Special Forces Hideout

The LSF strategise from their secure bunker deep in the heart of the highlands. Whether perched on mountainous peaks, sprawled under a great boulder or in amongst the gorse and heather, the LSF are there, watching and waiting for the opportunity to strike on those that get in the way of their mission to eliminate The Capital forces.

The Institute of Technological Resistance Hydro Dam

Housing some of the best technical minds, the ITR take advantage of an abandoned hydro dam. Out of sight and out of mind, under a dusty veil the ITR extract what little electricity they can from the water supply to fuel their technological assault to bring down The Capital regime.

The Circle of Lazarus Sanctuary

Killing, stealing, extorting and indoctrinating all that they can. The Circle of Lazarus live and die for the cause of ascension. Hunting technological hardware, the COL praise their technological relics as they hack and augment their bodies to become ever stronger, with the ambition to convert and dominate all.

The Syndicate Mansion

Hiding in plain sight, The Syndicate hideout in a great mansion, manipulating and plotting their escapades in great luxury and security, as they profiteer from the islands inhabitants.

Dynamic Elements

Wildlife

Dynamic reactive wildlife roam the game world eager to avoid combat and disturbance, they will flee from danger acting as an intuitive clue, providing players with valuable information as to enemies nearby.

Tracking

Other players and wildlife will displace and flatten foliage, leaving deep muddy footprints in wet soil and mud, and even print subtle dusty marks in houses as they explore the gameplay. Use this to your advantage and be on the lookout! However, you too will leave your marks on the world, so be careful who might be following you.

As you persist in the Proving Grounds, your actions have consequences. Taking excessive damage will cause you to leave a subtle blood trail for a brief period of time, this can be cut short by the use of a healing item such as a bandage. However, upon consuming the bandage the player will leave a packet on the floor, providing clues and telling a story to other inhabitants of the game world.

Comprehensive Destruction

Comprehensive destruction lies throughout, from fragmented trees to fragile plaster stud walls. This truly interactive world will challenge and reward you like never before.

Weather

A game world that feels truly alive and dynamic, as the sun sets low altitude fog rolls into a twilight night illuminated by a full moon. If muzzle flashes were compromising in daylight, they certainly are now. Prepare by sourcing a Flash-Hider attachment, but compromise on the absence of other muzzle attachments.

Time-of-Day

Achieved with a fully real-time lighting system and a comprehensive Time-of-Day weather system, Mavericks brings an exciting variety of opportunities to gameplay than ever before.

Mission Statements

Empowering World

Scaling gigantic mountains to rocky ravines, tracking players through dense gorse bush, or avoiding a roaming deer. Mavericks is built to be an empowering and realistic game world.

Beauty

Mavericks crafted breathtaking sceneries include sunsets over a dandelion meadow, to a tiresome dusty abandoned lumberyard.

Groundbreaking Scale

Pushing the boundaries of next-generation technology and feasible game worlds, Mavericks brings you a gameworld of truly epic proportions.
